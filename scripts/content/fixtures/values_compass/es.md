Values Compass (ES)
===================

1. Concept
----------
Values Compass helps people see their priorities and the tensions between them.

2. Values (10) with definitions
-------------------------------
1) FREEDOM (Freedom) - Choice, autonomy, and flexibility.
2) SECURITY (Security) - Safety, stability, and predictable outcomes.
3) ACHIEVEMENT (Achievement) - Progress, mastery, and goals.
4) CONNECTION (Connection) - Belonging, closeness, and trust.
5) GROWTH (Growth) - Learning, expansion, and novelty.
6) CONTRIBUTION (Contribution) - Helping others and making impact.
7) RECOGNITION (Recognition) - Status, visibility, and appreciation.
8) ENJOYMENT (Enjoyment) - Pleasure, play, and fun.
9) INTEGRITY (Integrity) - Honesty, principles, and authenticity.
10) ORDER (Order) - Structure, rules, and reliable methods.
